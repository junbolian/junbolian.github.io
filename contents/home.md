**Junbo Lian** is currently pursuing the prestigious Master's in Machine Learning and Data Science (MLDS) at Northwestern University, USA. His undergraduate thesis was recognized as an *outstanding graduation thesis*. His research interests include Operations Research, Artificial Intelligence-Generated Content (AIGC), Computer Vision (CV), Evolutionary Computation, and Mathematical Modeling. His entrepreneurial spirit led him to invent a Chinese herbal medicine mold detection system, establishing Hangzhou Boshiwei (Bossway) Testing Technology Co., Ltd., which garnered extensive media coverage. He currently serves as the Chief Analytics Officer at Wenzhou Buyi Pharmacy, a national top-100 pharmaceutical retail enterprise. He serves as a reviewer for internationally recognized journals, including *Journal of Advanced Research*, *Engineering Applications of Artificial Intelligence*, *Scientific Reports*, *Expert Systems With Applications*, and *Computers in Biology and Medicine*. Junbo has published multiple papers in prominent journals such as *Expert Systems with Applications*, *Applied Soft Computing*, *Computers in Biology and Medicine*, and *International Journal of Systems Science*, with two papers recognized as *ESI Hot Papers* (top 0.1%) and three as *ESI Highly Cited Papers* (top 1%). He was honored with the *National Scholarship* in 2024 and has received over ten prestigious awards at provincial and national levels. He also authored the book *Generative Foundation Models: A Comprehensive Beginner's Handbook* published on Amazon.
